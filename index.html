Introduction

Graduated from Staffordshire University with a 2:2 BSc Honours in Computer Games Programming with a major focus on programming in C++.

My main focus when working on projects is creating algorithms revolving around Procedural Generation that create a unique gaming experience for all players whether seasoned gamers or others looking to pick up and play for short periods of time. The idea of random gameplay sequences excites me and creating them even more with all the problem-solving that is involved in the creation. I've mainly spent time programming on solo projects, only working in teams a small number of times, with the aim of improving all aspects of my programming skills. However the idea of working in a team to create large-scale projects is exciting and what pushes me to continue to strive on improving myself

When playing games in my own time, i mainly aim towards genres utilising PCG or difficult gameplay namely the Souls-Like series with rewarding gameplay through determination and persistence to win. My main source of inspiration being Enter The Gungeon which utilises both of these aspects, and a few others like moonlighter which add unique twists in addition to the dungeon crawling experience.
